About Thori Hub

Established in 2020, Thori Hub is a proudly local ice cream company based in Thori-2, Parsa, Nepal. Founded by Hari Parajuli, Thori Hub was created with a passion for delivering refreshing, high-quality ice cream to the community, using locally-sourced ingredients and a commitment to great taste.

From a small beginning, Thori Hub has grown into a beloved brand in the region, known for its rich flavors, creamy textures, and friendly service. Whether it's a sunny afternoon treat or a special celebration, our ice creams have become a go-to choice for families, friends, and visitors alike.

At Thori Hub, we believe that ice cream brings people together— and every scoop tells a story of dedication, freshness, and local pride.
